About Us


Cleo's Candles is a mum-and-daughter start up. The concept to create Cleo's Candles began with Cleo making candles as a nice present for Cleo's school teachers at the end of 2022. Candles with unique scents - such as Chanel Type No. 5, Coconut and Lime and Champagne and Strawberries - were made as Christmas gifts for teachers, friends and family and the feedback was overwhelmingly positive. Hence, the plan arose to create a viable home business making exquisite candles that are individually handcrafted in luxury scents. When you buy a candle from Cleo's Candles you are helping a young 9-year-old girl fulfil her dream of becoming an entrepreneur.

Cleo's Candles is also a social enterprise. In 2023, Cleo ran a fundraiser at her school and donated $310 to charity.
